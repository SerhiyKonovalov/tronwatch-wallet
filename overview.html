Basic Operations and Compatibility

TronWatch offers essential functions for managing TRON assets, including token transfers, balance monitoring, and viewing transaction history. It supports both standard and custom tokens, as well as digital collectibles native to the TRON blockchain.

The wallet is designed for seamless compatibility with ongoing TRON updates, requiring no additional plugins or external software. It simplifies routine asset management tasks while allowing users flexibility to explore more advanced features when needed.
